Our Story

La Monje, founded by Yeabkal Demissew and Bethlehem Ghidey, isn't just a creative firm;
it's a data-driven storytelling powerhouse. We combine strategic analysis with captivating narratives to craft
impactful events curate compelling multimedia content, and develop innovative marketing campaigns.

Our Structure

We operate at the intersection of creativity and data to craft impactful events, compelling multimedia content,
and strategic marketing campaigns. This two-pronged approach ensures our work resonates with,
target audiences and drives tangible results for our clients

Our Approach

: We believe powerful storytelling is the key to forging connections and achieving results.
We combine strategic data analysis with captivating narratives. We're not just storytellers;
we're passionate creatives who translate your vision into experiences that resonate and drive results.

Our Model

: We operate under a Management by Objectives (MBO) model. We collaboratively set clear,
measurable goals with our clients at the outset of each project. This ensures alignment between
our efforts and their desired outcomes, fostering transparency and driving results.
